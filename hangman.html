Hangman Game

Welcome to the Hangman game! In this game, you will be presented with Java programming equations and hidden words, and your goal is to solve them to guess the correct letters in the hidden word. Here's how to play:

1: Click "Start" to get to pick the game.

"2": Equation: Solve the equation to reveal a letter in the hidden word.

"2": Word guessing: Guess a letter by clicking on the keyboard to reveal it.

3: You have a limit of six incorrect attempts before losing, so answer and guess them wisely.

4: Keep guessing letters until the word is revealed.

5: Click "Reset Game" to restart the game and generate a new hidden word or to start over on the equations.

Enjoy playing Hangman!
